Chocolate Chip Cookies

Satisfy your sweet tooth with the classic charm of Chocolate Chip Cookies.

This recipe ensures a perfect balance of chewiness and crunch, with each bite offering a delightful mix of soft center and chocolatey goodness. Using basic ingredients, this cookie recipe is perfect for both seasoned bakers and beginners alike. The simple steps and familiar ingredients make it a hassle-free treat to whip up in your own kitchen. Treat yourself to the joy of homemade cookies, whether you're sharing with loved ones or enjoying a quiet moment of indulgence. Happy baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour

  • ¾ tsp baking soda

  • ½ tsp salt

  • ¾ tsp cinnamon

  • ¾ cup butter, room temperature

  • 1 cup packed brown sugar

  • ½ cup white sugar

  • 2 eggs (at room temperature)

  • 1 tsp vanilla

  • 2 cups dark chocolate chunks

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

  2. In a medium mixing bowl add the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon, whisking together.

  3. In a large mixing bowl beat butter with brown sugar and white sugar. Scrape the sides of the bowl, add the eggs and vanilla, then beat until combined.

  4. A little at a time, add the flour mixture to the egg mixture, making sure to mix well with each addition.

  5. Once the flour is incorporated and cookie dough is formed, fold in the chocolate chunks.

  6. Drop dough by rounded teaspoons onto an ungreased cookie sheet, about 2 inches apart.

  7. Bake for 8-9minutes, letting the edges turn light brown. Transfer to wire rack to cool.

    8. Pro Tip: Letting the butter and eggs come to room temperature is a key step in baking amazing cookies.

    Instead of chocolate chunks, you can get creative with whatever you desire. Other delicious add ins include pecans/walnuts with chocolate chunks, your favorite candy bar chopped into small chunks, or M&M/Resees Pieces.
