Sourdough Loaf

Experience the Delight of Freshly Baked Sourdough.

Our sourdough is carefully and skillfully made using traditional methods. This results in a tangy flavor and a light airy texture that's sure to please. Whether you're making sandwiches or simply enjoying it with butter or a spread, our sourdough is a delicious testament to the art of baking.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup warm water

  • 2 ½ tbsp butter, melted

  • 2 ¾ tsbp honey or cane sugar

  • ½ tbsp salt

  • ½ cup active sourdough starter

  • 5 cups flour

    • Baker's note: Active starter is sourdough starter that has been fed within the last 8 – 10 hours or is nice and bubbly.

Instructions

  1. With a wooden spoon, mix together warm water, melted butter, honey/sugar, salt, and active starter. Mix in flour 1 cup at a time. Once all of the flour has been added, scrape dough onto lightly flour surface and begin kneading with your hands to help form the dough.

  2. Once dough is formed, place in greased bowl, cover and allow to rise 5-12 hours.

  3. After allowing the dough to rise, form dough into loaf and place in lightly greased loaf pan. Allow to rise 3-4hrs.

  4. Bake bread at 375°F for 45 minutes. After baking is complete, allow the bread to cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then remove and place on cooling rack. Enjoy!
