Buttery Ribeye

Experience the Rich Flavor and Succulent Tenderness of a perfectly cooked Ribeye.

Each mouthwatering bite is a symphony of flavor, enhanced by the indulgent buttery flavor that melts right in your mouth. Take your time, savor, and let each bite leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak

  • Spice blend:
    • 1 tsp Salt
    • 1 tsp Black pepper
    • 1 tsp Parsley
    • 1 tsp Rosemary
    • 1 tsp Thyme

  • Garlic Honey Butter:
    • 5 tbsp softened butter
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • 4 garlic cloves minced

Instructions

  1. Combine salt, pepper, and herbs in a bowl.

  2. Season the steak on each side with the spice blend.

  3. Preheat the griddle on high (400°F).

  4. Add 1 tbsp garlic honey butter to griddle and allow to melt.

  5. Place the steak on the griddle once the butter sizzles and add another tbsp of garlic honey butter on top of the steak to melt while the first side cooks.

  6. Cook according to your preference for doneness:

    • For rare, cook for 2-3 minutes on each side and let rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing (120-125°F).

    • For medium-rare, cook for 3-4 minutes on each side and let rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing (130-135°F).

    • For medium, cook for 4-5 minutes on each side and let rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing (140-145°F).

    • For medium-well, cook for 5-6 minutes on each side and let rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing (150-155°F).

    • For well-done, cook for 6-7 minutes on each side and let rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing (160-165°F).
  7. Enjoy your perfectly cooked Ribeye steak!
