Sausage Gravy

Enjoy a savory breakfast experience with this hearty Sausage Gravy.

This comforting classic combines crumbled savory sausage with a creamy, seasoned gravy, creating a rich and satisfying topping. Whether you're enjoying it over freshly baked biscuits or as a delightful addition to your breakfast spread, this Sausage Gravy promises to add a touch of homestyle flavor to your plate. Embrace the delicious simplicity of this Southern-inspired favorite and elevate your breakfast to a whole new level.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound sage sausage

  • 1 cube beef bullion, crushed

  • 1 cup milk

  • ¼ cup all purpose flour

  • Salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste

Instructions

  1. Add sausage and crushed bullion cube to skillet, breaking up sausage as it browns.

  2. Once the sausage is cooked all the way, add the flour over top and mix.

  3. With stove top on medium high heat, slowly stir in milk. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Stir frequently and cook until gravy begins to thicken.

  4. Enjoy over a freshly baked buttermilk biscuit.
