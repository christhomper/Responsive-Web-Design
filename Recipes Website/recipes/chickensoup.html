Creamy Chicken and Rice Soup

Experience the flavors of homemade Creamy Chicken and Rice Soup that brings the warmth of comfort food to your kitchen.

In this easy-to-follow recipe, tender chicken, white rice, and a mix of veggies blend harmoniously in a creamy broth. It's a simple yet satisfying soup that's perfect for a comforting dinner at home. Give it a go and treat yourself to a bowl of homemade goodness!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp butter

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil

  • 1 pound boneless chicken thighs

  • ½ onion, diced

  • 3 cloves garlic minced

  • 2 celery stalks, diced

  • 2 large carrots, diced

  • 1 large portabella mushroom or 4oz baby portabella, diced

  • 2 cans (10.5oz) cream of mushroom soup

  • 4 cups chicken broth

  • 1 cup white rice

  • ½ Tbsp salt

  • 1 tsp black pepper

  • 1 tsp paprika

  • 1 tsp dried rosemary

  • 1 tsp dried thyme

  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper

  • 3 cups milk

  • 1/3 cup flour

Instructions

  1. In the instant pot, Sauté chicken with oil and butter until lightly browned. Add all vegetables and sauté for another 2-3 minutes.

  2. Turn off the sauté function. Add the spices, chicken broth, cream of mushroom soup, and rice to the instant pot and mix together.

  3. Pressure cook on HIGH for 30 minutes and allow for a 10-minute natural release.

  4. While pressure cooker is naturally releasing, heat milk in a small saucepan, stirring frequently to prevent burning. Once heated, slowly whisk in the flour.

  5. Add milk/flour mixture to the soup after natural release is complete and remaining pressure has been released.

  6. Ladle the soup into bowls and serve. Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week.

    7. Don't have an instant pot? This recipe does great on the stove top. Follow steps 1 and 2, sauteing in a large pot. After step 2, bring soup to a boil, then reduce heat to maintain a simmer. Simmer soup until rice is fully cooked, about 30 minutes.
