Chicken Tortilla Soup

Enjoy the comforting flavors of Chicken Tortilla Soup.

Enhanced with the zesty kick of Rotel, the smoky depth of fire-roasted diced tomatoes, and the heartiness of black beans and sweet corn, this soup is a medley of robust flavors and satisfying textures. Seasoned with a blend of chili powder, paprika, cumin, and a hint of cayenne pepper, each spoonful delivers a balance of spice and warmth.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp butter

  • 1 white onion, diced

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1½ pounds chicken, diced

  • 8 cups chicken broth

  • 1 can rotel

  • 1 can fire roasted diced tomatoes

  • 1 can black beans, drained

  • 1 bag frozen corn 12 oz.

  • 2 Tbsp chili powder

  • 2 tsp paprika

  • 2 tsp cumin

  • 1 tsp salt

  • 1 tsp black pepper

  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper

  • 1 avocado, chopped

  • ¼ cup cilantro, chopped

  • ½ cup green onions, diced

  • tortilla strips

Instructions

  1. In the instant pot, sauté onion, garlic, and chicken with butter until lightly browned.

  2. Turn off the sauté function and add the remaining ingredients.

  3. Pressure cook on HIGH for 8 minutes and allow for a 5 minute natural release, then quick release the remaining pressure.

  4. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with avocado, cilantro, green onions and tortilla strips as toppings. Enjoy!

Nutritional Information

Yields 8 servings
Serving size 2 cups
  • 341 calories

  • 16g fat

  • 24g carbohydrate

  • 26g protein
