Berry Baked Oatmeal

Experience the wholesome flavors of Berry Baked Oatmeal.

A delicious breakfast option that's both hearty and satisfying. Bursting with the natural sweetness of mixed berries and the comforting warmth of oats, this baked oatmeal is a great way to kick-start your morning. Pair it with a dollop of yogurt or a drizzle of maple syrup to enhance its flavors.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups oats

  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder

  • ½ tsp salt

  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten

  • 2 ½ cups milk

  • ½ cup maple syrup

  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

  • 3 tbsp butter, melted

  • 3 cups fresh berries (blackberries, blueberries, raspberries, strawberries(chopped))

  • Cinnamon to sprinkle on top

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉ and grease an 8 x 8 baking dish.

  2. Combine oats, baking powder, and salt in a medium sized bowl then place ½ of the oat mixture in the bottom of the greased dish.

  3. Next, add half of the berries to the baking dish and then add the remaining oats.

  4. In a small bowl whisk together eggs, milk, maple syrup, vanilla, and butter. Then pour into the baking dish, over the oat and berry layers. Gently shake the baking dish to allow the liquid to settle to the bottom.

  5. Add remaining berries and sprinkle the top with cinnamon. Bake for 25-40 minutes. Dish is done when the oatmeal is set.

Nutritional Information

Yields 8 servings
Serving size 1 cup
  • 278 calories

  • 8g fat

  • 42g carbohydrate

  • 8g protein
