Fried Rice

Indulge in the irresistible flavors of Benihana with this Fried Rice copycat recipe.

Succulent shrimp, perfectly cooked rice, and a medley of fresh vegetables come together in a symphony of savory perfection. Recreate the magic of Benihana right at home with this delicious and satisfying dish that captures the essence of their iconic shrimp fried rice. Elevate your culinary skills and treat your taste buds to a taste of restaurant-quality goodness!

Ingredients

  • Shrimp:
    • 1/2 pound of shrimp (or any preferred protein substitute)
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp black pepper
    • 1 tsp sesame oil

  • Rice:
    • Avoid using freshly steamed rice! Opt for rice that's at least a day old to prevent excess moisture. Too much moisture can result in soggy and mushy fried rice.
    • 6 cups cooked

  • Garlic Honey Butter:
    • 5 tbsp softened butter
    • 1 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tbsp honey
    • 4 garlic cloves minced

  • Sesame Oil:
    • While my preferred oil is sesame oil, you can also use canola or vegetable oil for a more neutral flavor profile.

  • 4 medium carrots diced (~ 1 ½ cups)

  • 1/2 white onion diced (~ 1 cup)

  • 3 scallions chopped

  • 2 eggs

  • 3 garlic cloves minced

  • Garlic Honey Butter (see above)

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare your rice the night before and leave it covered in the fridge to dry overnight or for up to a day for optimal results.

  2. Soften 5 tbsp of butter and mince 4 cloves of garlic. Add 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp honey, mix well and refrigerate until firm and ready to use.

  3. Marinate your chosen protein with 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp of sesame oil. Chop carrots, onions, and scallions, and mince garlic. Set aside.

  4. Heat 1 tbsp of garlic honey butter in a pan over high heat, cook your protein until done. Remove and set aside.

  5. In the same pan, heat 3 tbsp of garlic honey butter. Briefly fry garlic for 15 seconds, followed by carrots and onions for 1-2 minutes. Add the rice and continue frying, separating and combining until relatively dry (~ 3 minutes).

  6. Create a hole in the center of the rice to fry the eggs. Add 1 tbsp of garlic honey butter, eggs, and scallions cooking until done. Mix into the rice. Enjoy!
